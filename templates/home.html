Getting Started

Follow the steps below to configure the Radio Recorder:

  1. Ensure your radio station is created.
  2. Create your show.
  3. Manually put your JPEG artwork into the directory at debian-04.lan:/home/alw/code/radio_recorder_host/config/art.
  4. Schedule a cron job on debian-04.lan with crontab -e.
